Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how SoloScripted ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our games, website, and services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Services. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Usage Data: Information about your activity on our Services, such as puzzles played, features used, time spent, and game progress.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our Services, including IP address, operating system, browser type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Information from Third-Party Ad Partners: We work with third-party advertising partners (e.g., Google AdMob) to show ads in our Services. These partners may collect information, including device identifiers, location data, and demographic information, to provide personalized advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Services.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our Services.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our Services.
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • Serve advertisements, including personalized ads.
  • Communicate with you for customer service, to provide you with updates and other information relating to the Service, and for marketing and promotional purposes.

3. Advertising

Our Services are supported by third-party advertising. These ad networks may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to collect information to provide advertising that is more relevant to your interests. You can often opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings ("Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

4. Your Data Protection Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. We aim to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data.

  • The right to access, update or delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification. You have the right to have your information rectified if that information is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • The right to object. You have the right to object to our processing of your Personal Data.
  • The right of restriction. You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to data portability. You have the right to be provided with a copy of the information we have on you in a structured, machine-readable and commonly used format.
  • The right to withdraw consent. You also have the right to withdraw your consent at any time where we relied on your consent to process your personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain EEA countries). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.
