Crafting Digital Worlds

Welcome to SoloScripted, where individual mastery meets cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence to create captivating mobile and web games.

SoloScripted is a one-person studio dedicated to redefining the boundaries of interactive entertainment through the power of AI and innovative game design. Founded on the belief that a single developer, equipped with modern tools and a passion for creativity, can deliver experiences that rival those of larger teams, SoloScripted is committed to pushing the envelope in both mobile and web gaming.

About Us

At SoloScripted, we're reimagining what one passionate creator can build at the crossroads of gaming and AI.

Founded as a solo venture, SoloScripted thrives on the belief that innovation doesn’t require a big team—just big ideas, relentless focus, and a love for building things that people truly enjoy. We craft bold, story-rich mobile and web-based games that are more than just entertainment—they're living, evolving experiences powered by intelligent systems.

Why Solo?

Because creative freedom matters. Because being small means we can move fast, stay weird, and take risks big studios can’t. Every line of code, every piece of art, and every design decision comes from a singular vision—with no layers in between.

Our Vision

To build games that surprise you. Games that think. Games that remember.

We imagine a future where AI helps craft truly dynamic worlds—where your choices matter not just for one session, but across time. With SoloScripted, we’re laying the groundwork for that future, one project at a time.
